About PDF Watermarking

Adding watermarks to PDFs helps you:

  • Mark document ownership or classification
  • Indicate document status (e.g., DRAFT, FINAL, CONFIDENTIAL)
  • Add branding to your documents
  • Deter unauthorized copying or distribution

How It Works

Our watermarking process is simple but effective:

  1. Your PDF document is analyzed
  2. A semi-transparent diagonal watermark is applied to each page
  3. The watermark is positioned to minimize interference with content
  4. The watermarked PDF is generated for download
The watermark is designed to be visible but not overly distracting. For best results, use short text phrases.
